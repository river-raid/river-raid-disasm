6C52: Complete bonus life sound sequence
Resets the sound counter and clears the SOUND_BIT_BONUS_LIFE flag to stop the sound effect.
bonus_life_sound_done 6C52 LD A,$00 Reset counter to 0.
6C54 LD ($6C30),A
6C57 LD HL,$6BB0 Clear SOUND_BIT_BONUS_LIFE in sound_flags to indicate sound is complete.
6C5A RES 4,(HL)
6C5C RET
