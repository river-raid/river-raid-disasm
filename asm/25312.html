62E0: Decrease vertical coordinate of the object by the value of 5F64.
Subtracts the current scroll delta from an object's Y coordinate. Used to reverse the adjustment made by 62DA after collision checking.
Input
B Current Y coordinate
Output
B New Y coordinate (B - scroll_delta)
62E0 LD A,($5F64) Load scroll delta from 5F64 and subtract from B.
62E3 LD H,A
62E4 LD A,B
62E5 SUB H
62E6 LD B,A
