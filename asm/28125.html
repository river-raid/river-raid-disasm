6DDD: Reset scrolling title text
Resets the scrolling text pointer to the beginning of the title text at 8182 and continues the overview loop.
6DDD LD HL,$8182 Reset text pointer 5F7E to 8182 (start of title text).
6DE0 LD ($5F7E),HL
6DE3 LD A,$00
6DE5 LD ($5F6D),A Clear 5F6D and jump back to overview_loop.
6DE8 JP $6D64
