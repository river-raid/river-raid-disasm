7296: Tank operation routine
Operates tanks on the river. Tanks move 2 pixels per frame and signal their center position ($80) to suppress the bank tank shell. Tanks on the river bank are handled separately via 7302.
  • Skips processing every other frame (tick check)
  • Tanks on bank (bit 5 set) handled via 7302
  • River tanks move left/right, set TANK_SHELL_ACTIVE at X=$80
  • Uses XOR blending mode for rendering
