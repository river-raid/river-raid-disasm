66EE: Update bridge Y position during scroll
Used by the routine at 66D0.
Adjusts the bridge's Y position (5F6E) based on current scrolling. If no bridge exists (Y=0), returns immediately. If the bridge scrolls off the bottom of the screen (Y AND $88 == $88), clears it.
66EE LD A,($5F6E) If no bridge, return; otherwise advance bridge Y position.
