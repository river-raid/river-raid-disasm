Missile fire
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 8A02 when the player fires a missile.
Engine (normal speed)
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 6C5D during normal speed flight (player not pressing up or down).
Engine (fast speed)
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 6CB8 when the player holds up to accelerate.
Engine (slow speed)
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 6CD6 when the player holds down to decelerate.
Explosion
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 6C7B when the player's plane is destroyed. The ON delay varies semi-randomly between frames because the DE register is not intentionally set by the caller, giving the explosion its noisy character. Each frame plays 4 cycles of waveform with a decreasing OFF delay, making the sound more rapid and urgent as the explosion progresses.
Low fuel warning
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 6CF4 when the fuel level drops below the warning threshold. The pitch warbles by decrementing the period each frame, creating an urgent rising tone that resets after reaching zero.
Bonus life
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 6C31 when the player earns an extra life. It generates a rising pitch over 63 frames by calling the ROM BEEPER routine at $03B5 with decreasing pitch values.
Tank full
This sound effect is produced by the routine at 6E92 when the fuel tank is already full and cannot accept more fuel. It plays a short beep (~1450 Hz, ~6ms) via the ROM BEEPER routine.
